Our Story

We're on a mission to make mental healthcare accessible for all

Founded in 2020, MindCare has helped over 50,000 individuals access quality mental health support — anytime, anywhere.

Our Mission

To break down barriers to mental healthcare by providing affordable, accessible, and evidence-based support to everyone — regardless of location, income, or background.

Our Vision

A world where mental health is prioritized equally with physical health — where everyone has a trusted companion on their wellness journey.

"Mental health is not a luxury — it's a fundamental human right. We're building the infrastructure to make quality care available to everyone."

— Alex Morgan, Founder & CEO

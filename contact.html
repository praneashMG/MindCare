Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly will I get a response?

Our support team typically responds within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, please use our live chat or call our support line.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. MindCare is HIPAA compliant and uses 256-bit encryption. Your privacy is our top priority. We never share your personal information without consent.

Do you offer partnerships for companies?

Yes! We offer enterprise plans for companies of all sizes. Contact our partnerships team at partners@mindcare.io for a custom quote.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. No cancellation fees, and you'll have access until your billing period ends.

Do you accept insurance?

We are working with major insurance providers. Currently, we accept HSA/FSA payments. Please contact us to check if your plan is covered.
